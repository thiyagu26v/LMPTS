# Learner Guide

Self-service enrollment and progress tracking for students.

## Register Your Profile

1. Go to **Learner Portal**
2. Enter your Learner ID, Name, and Email
3. Click **Register**

## Find Available Courses

1. Select your Learner ID
2. Click **Load Progress**
3. Check **Suggested courses** — these are courses you can enroll in now

## Enroll in a Course

1. Enter the course code (e.g., CS102)
2. Click **Validate** first
3. If validation passes, click **Enroll**

## Track Your Progress

The progress bar shows your completion rate. The enrollment table lists all your courses with status and scores.

## Complete a Course

After finishing coursework:
1. Enter the course code
2. Optionally enter your score (0-100)
3. Click **Mark Complete**
4. New courses may appear in Suggested courses

## Plan Your Learning Path

Use the **Learning Paths** tab to find the optimal sequence from one course to another (e.g., CS101 to CS301).

## Common Issues

- **"Missing prerequisites"** — Complete required courses first (shown in error message)
- **"Already enrolled"** — You're already in this course
- **"Course not published"** — Course isn't available yet; contact admin
